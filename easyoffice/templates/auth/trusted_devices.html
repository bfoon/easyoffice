About Trusted Devices

When you sign in from a new device and successfully verify the OTP code emailed to you, that device is added here so you don't have to verify again every time.

Trusted devices expire automatically after a set period — after which OTP will be required again on that device.

If you see a device you don't recognise, revoke it immediately and change your password.
